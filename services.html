Custom Clearance

We provide customs clearance services to all our clients and products stored at our oil terminal, the customs clearance work involves preparation and submission of documentations required to facilitate export or imports of petroleum and petrochemical products into any country where our clients need, representing clients during customs examination, assessment, payment of duty and co taking delivery of products from customs after clearance along with the required documents.

Pipeline Distribution

With a vast majority of pipeline network and partnership with leading petroleum distribution companies we have created an ease of access to pipeline networks linking major distribution channels, storage units and terminals. One of the main objectives of our company is to achieve the greatest possible value creation from the extraction of petroleum resources, refining of these products through our partners and the distribution of these products to our clients globally.
