Our Services

Custom Clearance

We provide customs clearance services to all our clients and products stored at our oil terminal, the customs clearance work involves preparation and submission of documentations required to facilitate export or imports of petroleum and petrochemical products into any country where our clients need, representing clients during customs examination, assessment, payment of duty and co taking delivery of products from customs after clearance along with the required documents.

Pipeline Distribution

With a vast majority of pipeline network and partnership with leading petroleum distribution companies we have created an ease of access to pipeline networks linking major distribution channels, storage units and terminals. One of the main objectives of our company is to achieve the greatest possible value creation from the extraction of petroleum resources, refining of these products through our partners and the distribution of these products to our clients globally.

Petroleum Storage

Our services in providing efficient storage systems, pipeline network, tanker fleets and vessels for the transporting oil and gas from the fields are an important element in this sector and requires a lot of efforts to achieve this. Petroleum products are used in every area of our lives which include gasoline, bunker fuel, steam coal, petroleum coke, LNG/LPG, and lubricants. These very few products touch every asspect of our lives from fueling our cars, heating our homes, e.t.c.

Value added Services

At UNKUDINSKAYA TANK FARM our management team plays a vital role in combining the strength of all of our clients, UNKUDINSKAYA TANK FARM accumulate the total freight volume, identifying the best local and long-distance carriers across all destinations then we negotiate discount rates. UNKUDINSKAYA TANK FARM passes the value of those discounts on to each individual client. Also providing Inventory Tracking by item number, product lot code or batch code, barcode or other specified fields noted.

Trade Office

Our Trade office department controls a trading platform where we trade a wide range of third party crudes, Aviation Kerosene Jet Fuel JP54, with 140 million barrels of crude delivered. We understand the difficulty in securing secure trading partners and hence do whatever it takes to ease the burden. In addition to continuous businesses development efforts in Europe, the America, Middle East and Asia through long term relationships, We are able to leverage all our services.

Supply chain management

Our supply chain management team provides the management of the flow of petroleum products and services, involves the movement and storage of refined and raw materials, of work-in-process inventory, and finished goods from point of origin to point of consumption. Our SCM team is the active management of supply chain activities to maximize customer value and achieve a sustainable competitive advantage. This is a conscious effort by our company.
